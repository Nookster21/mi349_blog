Beginner Crochet Patterns

Ready to start on a new/first project? You are in the right place :) In the list below, you’ll find beginner-friendly crochet patterns that are simple and easy to follow. You can start with the simplest pattern and work your way up to more difficult ones as you gain experience. So whether you’re a total beginner or you’ve been crocheting for years, we’ve got a pattern that will suit your needs.

Scarf

It's one of the first pieces I have made to master basic stitches. When you think about it, scarfs are just a long rectangle and is straight forward.

Kaizo ROM Hacks

Because of this infamous hack, Kaizo is now a subgenre of challenging levels that pushes the player, but have the balance of having fun. To play these types of hacks you have to have patience because every 15 seconds something will try to kill you. Luckily, there are more hacks that have a wide spectrum of difficulties, so players can start off somewhere. When watching a skilled player play SMW ROM hack, it looks very easy, but trust me when I say it's one of the hardest games I have ever played.
